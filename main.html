The Automobile

A car or automobile is a motor vehicle with wheels. Most definitions of cars say that they run primarily on roads, seat one to eight people, have four wheels, and mainly transport people (rather than goods)


Cars were invented in 1886, when German inventor Carl Benz patented his Benz Patent-Motorwagen. The original cost of the vehicle in 1886 was 600 imperial German marks, approximately 150 US dollars (equivalent to $4,524 in 2021).


Karl's wife Bertha demonstrated its feasibility in a trip from Mannheim to Pforzheim in August 1888, shortly before it became the first commercially available automobile in history in the late summer of 1888.


Due to the creation of the Patent-Motorwagen, Benz has been hailed as the father and inventor of the automobile
